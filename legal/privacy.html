About This App

The VIGICAR Rent mobile application allows customers to view available cars, read car details, fill in a booking form, and generate a rental contract in PDF format that is stored locally on the device. The app runs without accounts and does not send your personal data to our servers.

No Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive data on external servers. All booking details you enter (name, phone number, pickup date, pickup time, location, and rental options) stay only on your device and are used exclusively to create the PDF contract.

  • No analytics
  • No ads or trackers
  • No background network calls

Local Functionality

The app works fully offline for browsing, booking, and PDF generation. The rental contract is saved locally in your device storage. You are responsible for printing, signing, and presenting the contract when picking up the car.

No data is uploaded to our servers. If your device uses system-level backups (for example, cloud backups), the handling of your locally saved PDF follows your device settings.

Data Retention and Control

Your booking inputs are used only to generate the PDF contract on your device. The PDF stays on your local storage until you delete it or uninstall the app, after which it is removed from the device. We do not keep copies of your data.

If you reinstall the app, previous locally stored PDFs are not restored unless they were backed up by your device settings.

Google Play Data Safety Summary

Data shared: None. Data collected: None. All data you enter stays on your device to generate the rental PDF.

  • Processing: On-device only; no background network calls.
  • Storage: Rental PDF saved locally; deletion is under your control.
  • Permissions: Uses storage/file access solely to save the PDF you request.
  • Communication: Dialer, WhatsApp, and Maps open only when you tap; no automatic sending.

This description matches the Google Play Data Safety section for VIGICAR Rent.

User-Initiated Communication

The app provides buttons that, when tapped by you, open your phone dialer, WhatsApp, or Google Maps using our public contact details. These actions are optional and occur only when you choose to start them. The app does not send or transfer your data in the background.

Third-Party Services

If you choose to interact with WhatsApp, your dialer, or Google Maps, those services are provided by third parties. Please review their privacy policies to understand how they handle your information.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for adults who rent vehicles. We do not knowingly collect or process information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to meet legal requirements or improve clarity. The latest version will always be available inside the app and on our official website. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the updated terms.
